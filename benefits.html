Benefits

What is the Charlie Ball?
The Charlie Ball is a revolutionary redesign in manual massage therapy. Based on the concepts of trigger point therapy and myofascial release, it was designed to provide relief from back pain associated with muscle knots, tension and tightness. Customizable and portable, the Charlie Ball can be used virtually anytime, anywhere to make everyday life a little easier.


Convenient:The Charlie Ball attaches to almost any chair - which means pain relief, any time, anywhere. Unlike other products on the market today, the Charlie Ball addresses the problem where it is most likely to occur - at your desk, in the car or on a plane.


Easy to Use:By using your own weight as the means of producing pressure it eliminates the hand and muscle fatigue that is associated with the use of other products. More than one kink in your back? No problem, once The Charlie Ball strapped to your chair, it’ll move freely, so all you need to do is slide it into position.


Customizable:You’re unique, so is your pain. The Charlie Ball will come with shells in two densities - hard and soft. You can also combine one half of each shell to create a medium density. You can control the pressure from the Charlie Ball by changing the shell. Initially, we recommend starting with the soft shell. Future generations of the Charlie Ball will include variations in shell density and shape and even custom color options, letting you tailor the Charlie Ball to fit your needs and personality. Making it the perfect design compliment to your home, office or any other place you might need it.


Always there for you: Need to go grab something? The Charlie Ball will still be in that ideal position when you get back. The Charlie Ball’s ability to remain suspended in that “perfect spot” allows you to focus on other tasks. Need a break? The Charlie Ball’s patented design allows you to simply slide it off to side until you need it again.


You have better things to do: Other massage products take dedicated time. Why spend 20 minutes lying on the floor or wrestling with other handheld products trying to relieve every day aches and pains? With the Charlie Ball you can spend all day treating yourself and feel better getting out of your chair then when you got in.


Compact: The Charlie Ball’s lightweight design makes it easy to carry, simply toss into your purse or carry-on and go.


Attaches to Almost Anything: The Charlie Ball’s patented locking hook and loop features offers you a multitude of attachment options so you can get relief wherever you are.


Multi-Use: The Charlie Ball also works for sore feet, tight hips and wrist pain. Throw it on the ground, strap it to a wall, the options are limitless.
