Meet the team

Michael

Michael is the founder and inventor of the Charlie Ball. With over ten years experience in the health and fitness industry he had the opportunity to help numerous people recover from from all types of injuries. Having suffered numerous traumatic injuries to his back has given him first hand knowledge of how not only to alleviate the everyday pain but also how to continue to push the boundaries of the activities he loves.

Michael created the Charlie Ball as way to achieve the benefits associated with many of today's acupressure/self-myofascial release products in a manner that was convenient to his hectic lifestyle.

Rolling around on the floor for 20 minutes to recover from a 1/2 car ride just became so time consuming. I knew I could create a better way. Charlie

Charlie is our unofficial mascot. She is also partly responsible for the Charlie Ball becoming a reality. We adopted her when she was 6 months old and 2 weeks before we had to attend a wedding in Bend Oregon. With no desire to leave her behind, we decided to make the drive from san Francisco to bend so that she could come with us. Not being able to sit in a car for more than 1/2 hr at a time, before my back started acting up, I was now facing an 8 1/2 hr drive both ways over the course of 3 days. To make a long story short I came up with an idea that eventually became the first prototype. The effects were truly amazing. I made the drive without stopping, except for gas, and felt great getting out of the car at the end of the trip.

As they say the rest is history.
