ATUS, Corp was founded in 2016 in San Francisco, CA. Their priority is to ensure that they create products that are safe, reliable and beneficial to people’s lives. Of utmost importance to them is customer satisfaction and bringing people products that meet their daily demands. Their mission is to ease the pain and stress associated with common musculoskeletal problems with products that are complementary to one’s daily lifestyle. This is accomplished by creating high quality products that are effective, affordable, efficient and that blend seamlessly into their customers’ daily routines. Their goal is to be recognized for “Making every day a little easier”.
